Quiz Instructions

  • There are 5 available quiz below where you can take one quiz at a time.
  • Each quiz consists of 10 questions.
  • For each correct answer you will be awarded 1 mark.
  • After clicking "Start the Quiz" button your timer will start and at the end of the quiz you can see the time taken in seconds to complete the quiz.
  • Questions which are not attempted will be considered as wrong answers.
  • After finishing the quiz you can see your results in quiz results page.
  • By Clicking on "End Quiz" you will be redirected to the starting page.
  • You can take other available quiz in the same manner or again try the same quiz.
